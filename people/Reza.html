Reza Abbasi-Asl


Reza Abbasi-Asl is a PhD student in the electrical engineering and computer sciences department at UC Berkeley, advised by Prof. Bin Yu. He received his MS in biomedical engineering from Sharif University of Technology in 2013 and earned his BS in electrical engineering from Tehran Polytechnics in 2010. Reza is interested in statistical modeling and signal processing, computational neurosciences, machine learning and data analysis especially in vision sciences.
