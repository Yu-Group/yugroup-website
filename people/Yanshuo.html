Yan Shuo Tan


I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at UC Berkeley's Foundations of Data Analysis Institute. From Spring 2020, I will be a Neyman Visiting Assistant Professor at UC Berkeley's Statistics Department hosted by Prof. Bin Yu. Previously, I did my PhD in Mathematics at the University of Michigan, where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Roman Vershynin and Prof. Anna Gilbert. I am broadly interested in methodology, applications, and theory of modern data science.
