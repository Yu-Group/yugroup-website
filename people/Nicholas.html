Nicholas Altieri


Nick Altieri is a fifth year Ph.D student in Statistics co-advised by John DeNero and Bin Yu. He graduated from UC Berkeley in 2011 with degrees in Mathematics, Statistics, and Computer Science. His work has been in the areas of natural language processing applications to healthcare and medicine, domain adaptation, machine translation, and interactive machine learning.
