      Email Communication Protocol  
         
     
    1. When you receive an email (from another member in the group or from people outside of the group), please try to reply within one or two days, at least to confirm the receipt of the email.
    2. If you need more time to give a substantative answer, please acknowledge within one or two days and give a deadline on when you will be able to give the substantative answer. When the deadline arrives, it is your responsibility to send another email either with the substantative answer or an explanation on why the deadline is missed and ask for more time with a new deadline if needed.
    3. If the email discussion is on a project involving other people, always copy these people.
    4. If some ideas come from other members on the project, give them credits in the email when you mention these ideas. If there were meetings of a subgroup before emailing, say so with names of the subgroup in the email to the whole group.
    5. Apparently, the part on giving credits applies also to other forms of communcation such as face-to-face meetings.
    		  
           
           
           
      Tasks for Group Members  
         
      Weekly Meeting Organizer:  
       
    1. Set up an agreed slot of 1.5 hours and book 1011 Evans at the beginning of each semester;
    2. Send out weekly reminder emails;
    3. Arrange meeting contents and presenters;
    4. Organize short-term visitors' schedules to meet group members.
    		  
       
      Website and Email Manager: (Xiaoling Lu | xl_lu2002 [at] yahoo [dot] com [dot] cn)  
       
    1. Maintain group website, including: update cover group photo, group photo archive, people page and links to individual website, research descriptions, publication list;
    2. Add new group members' emails to yugroup@stat, visitors@stat or grads@stat if not already, women@stat if applicable.
    		  
       
      Event Organizer:  
       
    1. Take a group shot every new academic year;
    2. When members leave the group, organize group lunch, signing cards/presents;
    3. Assist Bin in organizing potluck at the end of academic years if occasions allow.
    		  
       
      Member:

    2. Present at least once in an academic year;
